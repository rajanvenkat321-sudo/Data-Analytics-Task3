## 📖 Overview
This repository contains the deliverables for **Task 3** of the **ApexPlanet Data Analytics Internship**. It involves a comprehensive deep-dive analysis of a sales dataset to calculate core KPIs, perform demographic customer segmentation, and build an interactive Power BI dashboard to extract actionable business insights.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Define and calculate core Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
- Segment customers by age to understand demographic purchasing behavior.
- Design a professional, interactive, two-page Power BI dashboard.
- Extract actionable insights and business recommendations.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- 💰 **Total Revenue:** $139,399,439.65
- 📦 **Total Orders:** 992
- 💳 **Average Order Value:** $140,523.63
- 👤 **Revenue Per Customer:** $147,201.10
- 🛒 **Total Quantity Sold:** 5,435

---

## 🔍 Deep Dive Analysis (Segmentation)
Customers were segmented into: Young Adults (18-30), Adults (31-45), Middle Age (46-60), and Seniors (61+).
- **Highest Revenue Segment:** Adults ($46.2M)
- **Highest Spending Segment (AOV):** Seniors ($145.6K)

---

## 🖼️ Dashboard Features
**Page 1: Executive Dashboard**
Features top-level KPI cards, geographic revenue distribution, monthly trends, and category performance.

**Page 2: Customer Segmentation Dashboard**
Focuses on demographic insights, showing product and category preferences broken down by age segment.

---

## 🔑 Key Insights
- **Adults and Middle-Aged** customers generate the vast majority of revenue, heavily favoring Laptops within the Electronics category.
- **Seniors** have the lowest order volume but the highest Average Order Value, with a strong preference for Books.
- **Young Adults** predominantly purchase Mobiles.

---

## 💡 Conclusion
By understanding these demographic trends, the business can execute highly targeted marketing campaigns, optimize inventory based on regional demographic concentration, and improve cross-selling strategies to boost overall profitability.
